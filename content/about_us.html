Time for you to know a bit about us!

Visionary Intelligence & Transport Network (VINET) was started in the year 2925 by Edward Jamison “Jamie” Sharp, under the name Visionary Intellgience Network having served 10 years as an Aviator in the Space Corp. of the Intergalactic Alliance Navy, to facilitate the security of convoys transporting goods between stations and planets.

The story about behind the company!

In 2037 his brother, Benjamin “Dallias” Sharp, and Jahaerys “Dibby” Dibnah , two successful businessman joined with Jamie, to create a joint company under the name Visionary Intelligence & Transport Network to provide security functions to the Network’s own trade routes and convoys as well as to provide secuirty to other companies.

VINET, outfited with fighters and dropships alike also have engaged in counter-terror and counter-piracy acitivities when these threaten the well-being of the Network or its customers.
